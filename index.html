ABOUT HIDDEN CUP

Hidden Cup (HC) is an Age of Empires 2 1v1 tournament hosted by T90Official. In the main event, the participants play under hidden identities. The casters and audience do not know which player is which, and the competitors do not know who they are up against. After the grand finals, the players and the winner will be revealed.
The Hidden Cups are the biggest 1v1 Age of Empires 2 tournaments to date, with the largest prizepools, sponsored by Microsoft since Hidden Cup 3.
